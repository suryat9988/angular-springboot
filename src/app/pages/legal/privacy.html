Privacy Policy

BowerBox (“we”, “us”) operates innovatedquantum.com. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Who this applies to

BowerBox is intended for adults (18+) planning dates with a partner. If you are under 18, do not use this service.

Information we collect

  • Account data: Gmail address, display name, and Google account identifier when you sign in.
  • Date plans: Titles, descriptions, dates, locations, budgets, notes, and related planning details you save.
  • Partner chat: Messages you send to a connected partner inside the app.
  • Partner linking: Invite codes and connection metadata used to pair two accounts.
  • App activity: Game scores, rewards points, and relationship preferences stored for app features.
  • Device data: Push notification tokens if you allow browser notifications.
  • AI chat (optional): Messages you send to the planning assistant to help fill date plan fields.

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Store and display your date plans and partner messages.
  • Provide weather notes, rewards, lucky games, and planning features.
  • Send optional reminders about upcoming dates.
  • Improve reliability, security, and user experience.

Third-party services

We use trusted providers to run BowerBox. They process data according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, file storage, and messaging.
  • Google Sign-In — secure login with your Gmail account.
  • Open-Meteo — weather forecasts based on location and date you provide.
  • Google Gemini (optional) — AI-assisted date planning if enabled.
  • GitHub Pages — hosting for the public website.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers listed above, when required by law, or with your connected partner for features you choose (partner chat and shared planning).

Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your choices and rights

  • Sign out at any time from the app toolbar.
  • Disconnect a partner from Partner Chat.
  • Disable browser notifications in your device or browser settings.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing us.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as GDPR or CCPA. We will honor valid requests within a reasonable time.

Security

We use HTTPS, Firebase security rules, and Gmail-only sign-in to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International users

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.

Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: privacy@innovatedquantum.com
